BBB Image Forge

An all-in-one BeagleBone Black imaging appliance: choose a certified setup, enter your device details, insert an SD card, and let the app do the rest. No terminal, no Python setup, and no manual image paths.

What This App Does For You

  1. Requests a certified generic BBB image for the profile you choose.
  2. Applies your hostname, account, SSH, and network settings locally.
  3. Detects the SD card you just inserted and blocks risky targets.
  4. Flashes the card and keeps a support bundle if something fails.

Zero-Touch Promise

  1. The main app stays unprivileged.
  2. The helper handles only device and image operations.
  3. Your password, SSH key, and Wi-Fi never leave this machine.
  4. Only certified profiles and add-ons appear in the default flow.
